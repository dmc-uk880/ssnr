About The Club

The Chertsey S.S.N.R. Club, standing for **Soldiers, Sailors, and National Reserve**, serves as a dedicated social hub for veterans, service personnel, and the wider Chertsey community. We are proud of our heritage and strive to provide a supportive, welcoming environment.

We host a variety of activities, including social nights, celebratory functions, and community events, maintaining the tradition of fellowship and camaraderie associated with the services. Our mission is to ensure the club remains a thriving, affordable, and accessible resource for all members. **This page is an unofficial fan-made resource to highlight the club's activities.**
