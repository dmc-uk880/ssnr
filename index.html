About The Club

The Chertsey **SS&NR Club** is a long-standing **Private Members' Club**, proudly serving the community and those connected to the **Soldiers, Sailors, & National Reserve** heritage since the early 20th century. We are dedicated to providing a high-quality, welcoming social environment.

Our spacious and well-maintained facilities make us the perfect location for socializing, entertainment, and private functions. Visitors can expect fantastic value across our well-stocked bar and enjoy the traditional, friendly atmosphere that defines our club.

Club Facilities include:

Large Seated Area
Room for Hire
Snooker Area
Outdoor Garden & BBQ
Dedicated Darts Area
Large Screen Sports (Big Games)
