About The Club

The Chertsey S.S.N.R. (Social, Sports, and Recreational) Club is a non-profit community venue dedicated to hosting a wide variety of local activities. From social nights and darts leagues to charity events and family gatherings, we strive to be a welcoming and inclusive space for all residents of Chertsey and surrounding areas.

Our mission, inspired by the community spirit seen on the official Facebook page, is to ensure the club remains a thriving, affordable, and accessible place where community bonds are strengthened. **This page is a fan-made resource to highlight the fantastic work and events hosted here.**
