About The Club

The Chertsey SS&NR Club is a long-standing Private Members' Club, proudly serving the community and honouring its heritage of the **Soldiers, Sailors, & National Reserve**. We are dedicated to providing a high-quality, welcoming social environment.

Our spacious and well-maintained facilities make us the perfect location for socialising, entertainment, and private functions. Visitors can expect fantastic value across our well-stocked bar and enjoy the traditional, friendly atmosphere that defines our club. We are easily accessible and have a private car park.

Club Facilities include:

Large Seated Area
Room for Hire
Snooker Area
Outdoor Garden & BBQ
Dedicated Darts Area
Large Screen Sports (Big Games)
